● FAQs

Everything you need to know

Is Invisalign® as effective as traditional metal braces?+
Yes. For most alignment cases Invisalign® delivers results comparable to braces, with the added benefit of being removable and nearly invisible.
How long does the average Invisalign® treatment take?+
Most patients complete treatment in 12–18 months, though timelines vary based on your individual goals and starting alignment.
Will I need to come in for frequent appointments?+
One of the best parts of Invisalign® is the flexibility. Unlike metal braces that require frequent tightening, you'll typically only need a quick check-in every 6 to 8 weeks to ensure your progress is on track.
Are there any food restrictions with Invisalign®?+
None at all. Simply remove your aligners before eating, enjoy your meal, then brush and pop them back in.
Does Dream Smile Dental offer financing for Invisalign®?+
Yes — we offer flexible payment plans and simple financing through Cherry to make your dream smile affordable.
